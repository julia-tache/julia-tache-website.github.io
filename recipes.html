    Spencer's Extra Special Spaghetti Tacos

    Wanna impress your dinner guests with a fast, fun, and delicious recipe? Spaghetti Tacos are your go-to. It is sure to put a smile on even the most refined diner.
      Ingredients:
      10 Taco Shells
      Marinara Sauce (Store-bought is fine)
      Taco Seasoning
      1 Pound of Linguini

      1. Bring 6 cups of water to a boil. Add Linguini and cook until Al Dente, about 7-10 minutes.
      2. Heat up marinara sauce and add taco seasoning.
      3. Mix together hot pasta with warm sauce and begin to fill taco shells with spaghetti taco goodness.
